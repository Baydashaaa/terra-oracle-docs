Terra Oracle Classic

A decentralized Q&A, governance and draw protocol built on Terra Classic — powered by LUNC.

Terra Oracle Classic allows community members, validators, and developers to surface privileged knowledge anonymously, participate in governance draws, and engage in on-chain communication. The protocol operates entirely on Terra Classic (columbus-5) with no centralized backend. All payments, draws, and messages are verifiable on-chain.

Oracle Q&A
Ask questions about Terra Classic governance, validators and market. 200,000 LUNC per question.
Oracle Draw

Protocol treasury funded by Q&A, Chat, and Draw fees. Transparent on-chain.

Oracle Q&A

Ask questions about Terra Classic governance, validators and market. Get answers from the community.

What is Oracle Q&A?

Oracle Q&A is an on-chain question and answer system. Anyone can submit a question by paying 200,000 LUNC. The payment is split automatically, and the question author receives 2 free Weekly Draw entries.

Payment split per question (200,000 LUNC):
100,000 LUNC → Weekly Draw Pool  ·  100,000 LUNC → Protocol Treasury  ·  +2 free Weekly Draw entries for author

DAO Chat

On-chain messages stored forever on Terra Classic. Every message costs 5,000 LUNC.

What is DAO Chat?

DAO Chat is a permanent on-chain messaging system. Every message is stored on Terra Classic blockchain and is publicly visible forever. The cost per message is 5,000 LUNC, which goes entirely to the Protocol Treasury.

100% of Chat fees → Protocol Treasury

Earn Weekly Draw entries

Free Weekly Draw entries

In addition to NFT entries, participants earn free Weekly Draw entries through Terra Oracle activity:

ActivityFree entriesLimit
Every 10 DAO Chat messages+1 Weekly entryMax 2 per day
Each Oracle Q&A question (200,000 LUNC)+2 Weekly entriesNo limit

Free entries are tracked automatically via on-chain transaction history, updated every hour, and combined with paid NFT entries into a single pool.

How winners are selected

Winners are selected using the on-chain block hash at draw time — fully transparent and verifiable by anyone on Terra Classic.

winner_index = BigInt(block_hash) % BigInt(total_entries)

Daily Draw — step by step

  1. All NFT mint transactions to the Daily Draw wallet in the last 24 hours are collected
  2. Ticket array is built: Common = 1 ticket, Rare = 5, Legendary = 10
  3. Latest on-chain block hash is fetched at 20:00 UTC
  4. winner_index = BigInt(block_hash) % BigInt(total_tickets)
  5. Winner receives 80% of the prize pool automatically on-chain
  6. 10% → Protocol Treasury · 10% stays as seeds for next round
  7. If fewer than 5 entries — draw is skipped, all funds carry to next round

Weekly Draw — step by step

  1. All NFT mint transactions to the Weekly Draw wallet in the last 7 days are collected
  2. Free entries from Oracle Q&A and DAO Chat are added to the pool
  3. Three unique winners are selected using the block hash:
    1st: sha256(block_hash + "0") % total → 48%
    2nd: sha256(block_hash + "1") % total → 20%
    3rd: sha256(block_hash + "2") % total → 12%
  4. 10% → Protocol Treasury · 10% stays as seeds for next round
  5. If fewer than 5 entries — draw is skipped, all funds carry to next week

Daily Streak

The Daily Streak system rewards consistent protocol activity. Every day you interact with Terra Oracle Classic through a paid action, your streak grows — unlocking REP multipliers, fee discounts, and exclusive Draw bonuses.

What counts as a streak day

Your streak advances by 1 each day you complete at least one of the following paid actions:

ActionCost
Ask a question (Oracle Q&A)200,000 LUNC (or discounted price)
Send a message (DAO Chat)5,000 LUNC
Enter Daily DrawNFT mint fee
Enter Weekly DrawNFT mint fee

Free actions such as answering questions, voting, or poll voting do not count toward your streak.

Grace period

Missing one day every 7 days does not break your streak. The system automatically applies a grace period — the missed day is skipped and your streak continues as normal. However, only one grace is allowed per 7-day rolling window. Missing two or more days in a row always resets the streak to 1.

Streak milestones & rewards

Reaching a milestone unlocks permanent bonuses that apply as long as your streak is active. The REP multiplier is applied to all reputation points you earn.

REP multiplier

The streak multiplier applies to your total base REP score. For example, at a 14-day streak (×1.5), if your base REP is 1,000 your displayed reputation becomes 1,500. The multiplier is only active while your streak is running — if your streak resets, your REP returns to the base value.

Question discount

At 7+ days streak, the question fee drops from 200,000 LUNC to 150,000 LUNC (25% off). This discount stacks with your rank discount — the system always applies whichever discount is higher. For example, an ARCHON rank user (20% off) with a 7-day streak receives 25% off, not 20%.

14-day free entries

When you first reach a 14-day streak, 50,000 LUNC is automatically transferred from the Protocol Reserve wallet to the Weekly Draw pool. This corresponds to 2 free entries (25,000 LUNC each — the price of a Common NFT). The transfer is processed by an automated script that runs every hour.

Trusted User status (30 days)

Reaching 30 consecutive days unlocks Trusted User status. This is a permanent achievement — even if your streak resets, the status and golden profile border remain. As a Trusted User, every Draw entry you make receives an automatic +1 bonus entry at no extra cost, as long as your current streak is 30 or more days.

Streak in Your Stats

Open Your Stats in the Reputation section to see your current streak, longest streak, today's status, REP multiplier, and milestone progress. Each milestone card shows the reward it unlocks. The status badge shows either Streak secured today (green) or Today not completed (orange) — use it as a daily reminder to stay active.

Streak reset

If you miss more than one day (outside the grace period), your streak resets to 1 on your next active day. Milestones already reached are never removed — one-time rewards such as the 14-day LUNC transfer and the Trusted User golden border are permanent. Only the active REP multiplier and Draw bonuses depend on your current streak length.
