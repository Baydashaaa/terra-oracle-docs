Terra Oracle Classic

A decentralized Q&A, governance and draw protocol built on Terra Classic - powered by LUNC.

Terra Oracle Classic allows community members, validators, and developers to surface privileged knowledge anonymously, participate in governance draws, and engage in on-chain communication. The protocol operates entirely on Terra Classic (columbus-5) with no centralized backend. All payments, draws, and messages are verifiable on-chain.

Oracle Draw

Two draws - Daily & Weekly. Mint Oracle Mask NFTs to enter. On-chain verifiable draws on Terra Classic.

Oracle Draw is not a lottery - it is a mechanism designed to stimulate on-chain economic activity.

Where to mint

Minting happens on draw.terraoracle.io, directly against the Oracle Mask CW721 contract. One transaction pays for the NFT, mints it to your wallet and routes the LUNC into the chosen prize pool — it either all succeeds or nothing is charged.

Free entries are tracked automatically via on-chain transaction history, updated every hour, and combined with paid NFT entries into a single pool.

How winners are selected

Winners are selected using the on-chain block hash at draw time - fully transparent and verifiable by anyone on Terra Classic.

winner_index = BigInt(block_hash) % BigInt(total_entries)

Daily Draw - step by step

  1. All Oracle Mask mints registered to the Daily pool since the last draw are collected from the contract’s on-chain mint events
  2. Ticket array is built: Common = 1 ticket, Rare = 5, Legendary = 10
  3. Latest on-chain block hash is fetched at 20:00 UTC
  4. winner_index = BigInt(block_hash) % BigInt(total_tickets)
  5. Winner receives 80% of the prize pool automatically on-chain
  6. 10% → Protocol Treasury · 10% stays as seeds for next round
  7. If fewer than 5 entries - draw is skipped, all funds carry to next round

Weekly Draw - step by step

  1. All Oracle Mask mints registered to the Weekly pool in the last 7 days are collected from the contract’s on-chain mint events
  2. Free entries from Oracle Q&A and DAO Chat are added to the pool
  3. Three unique winners are selected using the block hash:
    1st: sha256(block_hash + "0") % total → 48%
    2nd: sha256(block_hash + "1") % total → 20%
    3rd: sha256(block_hash + "2") % total → 12%
  4. 10% → Protocol Treasury · 10% stays as seeds for next round
  5. If fewer than 5 entries - draw is skipped, all funds carry to next week

What the contract guarantees

Atomic mint. Payment, NFT creation and the transfer into the Daily or Weekly pool are one transaction. If any step fails the whole transaction reverts — a wallet can never be charged without receiving the NFT.
No free mints. The CW721 minter is the contract itself, so the standard mint entry point is rejected for every external caller, including the admin. The only way to obtain an Oracle Mask is to pay the tier price.
Price is read from the chain. The interface queries the live tier price before signing, and the contract rejects any amount that does not match exactly (Wrong payment). Under- and overpayment are both impossible.
Burn tax. Terra Classic applies a 0.5% burn tax to the contract’s outgoing transfer, so a pool receives 99.5% of the mint price. The contract itself forwards 100% and keeps no balance.

Help

FAQ

Short answers to the questions that come up most often. If something is missing, ask in DAO Chat.

Minting

Where do I mint Oracle Mask NFTs?
Only on draw.terraoracle.io, directly from the Oracle Mask contract. There is no marketplace step and no address to send LUNC to manually — never send LUNC to a wallet address to mint.
What happens if my mint transaction fails?
Nothing is charged. Payment, NFT creation and the transfer into the prize pool are a single transaction: if any part fails, the whole thing reverts and the LUNC stays in your wallet. It is not possible to pay and not receive the NFT.
I minted but the NFT is not in My Bag yet
Give it a few seconds and reload — the page reads directly from the blockchain, so the NFT appears as soon as the block is confirmed. If you closed the tab immediately after signing, a background job reconciles the mint into the current round within about 10 minutes. Your NFT is safe either way: it is already in your wallet on-chain.
Is there a limit on how many I can mint?
No. You can mint any number of NFTs, in any tier, in the same round. Every NFT adds its own entries to the pool you selected at mint time.
Why did the prize pool receive slightly less than I paid?
Terra Classic charges a 0.5% burn tax on the outgoing transfer. The contract forwards 100% of your payment; the chain deducts the tax in transit, so a pool receives 99.5%. The contract keeps nothing for itself.

Draws

Do I need to do anything after minting to enter the draw?
No. The pool you choose at mint time — Daily or Weekly — is written into the NFT itself, and the entry is registered automatically. You will see the NFT marked as active for that draw in My Bag.
Is my NFT burned when I enter a draw?
No. The NFT stays in your wallet permanently. After the draw runs it is marked as having played, so it no longer counts toward new rounds, but you keep the token — it remains transferable and tradable.
What happens if fewer than 5 entries are in a round?
The draw is skipped and the entire pool carries over to the next round. Nothing is lost and no partial payout happens.
How do I know the draw is not rigged?
Winners are selected from the on-chain block hash at draw time, which nobody — including the protocol — can predict or influence in advance. The formula is published in the Oracle Draw section, every entry is an on-chain mint event, and every payout is an on-chain transaction. All of it can be replayed independently on Terra Finder.
Can I win more than once in the same weekly draw?
No. The three weekly places are awarded to three unique wallets — a single wallet cannot occupy more than one place in the same round.

NFTs & the contract

Can I transfer or sell my Oracle Mask?
Yes. The collection follows the standard CW721 interface, so transfers, approvals and marketplace listings all work the same way as with any other Terra Classic NFT.
What do the token numbers mean?
Token IDs are sequential per tier — common-1, rare-7, legendary-2 — and are displayed as Common #1, Rare #7 and so on. The number is assigned by the contract and can never repeat.
Can the contract be changed after deployment?
The contract keeps a migration admin so new mechanics can be added later — NFT crafting is next on the roadmap. A migration preserves the contract address, all existing NFTs and their metadata. Every upgrade is documented here and stays verifiable through the published code checksum.
Can anyone mint an NFT for free?
No. The contract itself is the CW721 minter, so the standard mint entry point is rejected for every external caller — including the protocol admin. Paying the exact tier price is the only path to an Oracle Mask.

Reputation & general

Does REP cost anything? Can I buy it?
REP is not a token and cannot be bought, sold or transferred. It is earned purely through activity — asking and answering questions, chat messages, upvotes received, and NFT mints — and it determines your rank, fee discounts and weekly reward share.
Which wallets are supported?
Keplr, Galaxy Station, Terra Station, Luncdash and other Terra Classic compatible wallets. You need LUNC in the wallet for both the action itself and the network gas fee.
Where can I verify everything myself?
Every wallet, mint and payout is public. Use Terra Finder for transactions, the Smart Contract section for direct chain queries and the reproducible build steps, and the Treasury section for the full wallet list.

Daily Streak

The Daily Streak system rewards consistent protocol activity. Every day you interact with Terra Oracle Classic through a paid action, your streak grows - unlocking REP multipliers, fee discounts, and exclusive Draw bonuses.

What counts as a streak day

Your streak advances by 1 each day you complete at least one of the following paid actions:

ActionCost
Ask a question (Oracle Q&A)200,000 LUNC (or discounted price)
Send a message (DAO Chat)5,000 LUNC
Enter Daily DrawNFT mint fee
Enter Weekly DrawNFT mint fee

Free actions such as answering questions, voting, or poll voting do not count toward your streak.

Grace period

Missing one day every 7 days does not break your streak. The system automatically applies a grace period - the missed day is skipped and your streak continues as normal. However, only one grace is allowed per 7-day rolling window. Missing two or more days in a row always resets the streak to 1.

Streak milestones & rewards

Reaching a milestone unlocks permanent bonuses that apply as long as your streak is active. The REP multiplier is applied to all reputation points you earn.

REP multiplier

The streak multiplier applies to your total base REP score. For example, at a 14-day streak (×1.5), if your base REP is 1,000 your displayed reputation becomes 1,500. The multiplier is only active while your streak is running - if your streak resets, your REP returns to the base value.

Question discount

At 7+ days streak, the question fee drops from 200,000 LUNC to 150,000 LUNC (25% off). This discount does not stack with your rank discount - the system always applies whichever discount is higher. For example, an ARCHON rank user (20% off) with a 7-day streak receives 25% off, not 45%.

14-day free entries

When you first reach a 14-day streak, 50,000 LUNC is automatically transferred from the Protocol Reserve wallet to the Weekly Draw pool. This corresponds to 2 free entries (25,000 LUNC each - the price of a Common NFT). The transfer is processed by an automated script that runs every hour.

Trusted User status (30 days)

Reaching 30 consecutive days unlocks Trusted User status. This is a permanent achievement - even if your streak resets, the status and golden profile border remain. As a Trusted User, every Draw entry you make receives an automatic +1 bonus entry at no extra cost, as long as your current streak is 30 or more days.

Streak in Your Stats

Open Your Stats in the Reputation section to see your current streak, longest streak, today's status, REP multiplier, and milestone progress. Each milestone card shows the reward it unlocks. The status badge shows either Streak secured today (green) or Today not completed (orange) - use it as a daily reminder to stay active.

Streak reset

If you miss more than one day (outside the grace period), your streak resets to 1 on your next active day. Milestones already reached are never removed - one-time rewards such as the 14-day LUNC transfer and the Trusted User golden border are permanent. Only the active REP multiplier and Draw bonuses depend on your current streak length.
